CFG Compilers

From its founding in 1970, CFG has designed programming languages and built compilers. These have ranged from special-purpose languages and compilers for seismic data processing for Societe National des Petroles d'Aquitaine, a predecessor of Total, to PL/M and Fortran compilers for the Intel Corporation.
In addition, CFG developed and marketed a number of language preprocessors and compilers. While none of these compilers are currently available, they are still in use on many button-up and other long-term projects. To aid in support of these projects, we include the documentation here:
